About Me

Welcome to my page!
As you can probably tell by my nav bar, my name is Pablo. I've always had a passion for technology, but my passion for software development started with a MatLab class that I took when I was finishing my first Bachelor's in Biomedical Engineering. I fell in love with the idea of being able to manipulate code to represent an idea and solve problems. I saw the potential that software development had to help hundreds or thousands of people across the world. I also enjoy challenges, so fixing bugs within code became an act that I enjoyed.

I love helping people. Ever since I was young, I've always found the idea of helping others to be one of the most satisfying actions that one could perform. Maybe it was the lessons I learned from watching all the Star Wars movies, but up to this day I believe that helping one another is the key to saving the world. I know that software development can help me achieve that and give me some interesting challenges along the way.
